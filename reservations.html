Reservations

We have a limited number of walk-on computers, but the best way to ensure a spot is by making a reservation. Reservations are not required, but they are highly encouraged to make sure you have a PC when you arrive at the CGA, as we cannot guarantee PC availability.

Reservations can be made on the website above for Wednesday to Saturday between the hours of 5pm and 11pm, with a maximum booking time of three hours.
